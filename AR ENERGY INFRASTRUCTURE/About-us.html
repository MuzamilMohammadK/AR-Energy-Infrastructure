OUR STORY

AREI one of the top players in the Solar sector in Hyderabad, striving diligently to utilize natural resources (solar energy in our case) for the betterment of the nation.

Solar energy is the naturally available source of energy, continuously provided by the Sun. It is then vital non-conventional source of energy and a non-polluting one that helps in creating a greenhouse effect.

Our Journey in Solar Paneling commenced in the year 2014 and our ardent efforts at par excellence have now acknowledged us as one of the top companies in Hyderabad in the specific sector.

Typically our solar panel would last for more than 30 years, substantially reducing global warming. This works towards saving electricity costs as well
